On the study of light and shade...

The codex details the nature of perspective and anatomy. It is a work of continuous observation. Herein we chart the observations as noted in the original manuscript.

Observations on the movement of water and mechanics are also gathered together in this collection, revealing a mind dedicated to endless inquiry.
